## Installations
- pip install dotenv pillow discord.py requests

## Usage

Once the bot is running and invited to your server:

- The bot will automatically send a custom welcome image to a specified channel whenever a new member joins.

## Customization

You can modify the background image by replacing `welcome_background.png` in the `Join Bot` folder.

## Permissions

Ensure the bot has the following permissions in your Discord server:

- Read Messages
- Send Messages
- Attach Files
